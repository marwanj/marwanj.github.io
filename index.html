My name is Marwan Jalaleddine and I am a second year Electrical Engineering Student Master's student at the McGill University. When I decided to study Electrical and Computer Engineering, I knew that I was signing up to solve the world's most complex problems and I was not going to settle for less.

After working on several distinct course projects in the different disciplines of electrical and computer engineering, I felt most passionate about signal processing, and hardware engineering. I want to use what I have learned throughout my studies to improve life on earth. This led me to work with Professor Warren Gross in his Integrated Systems for Information Processing (ISIP) lab.
